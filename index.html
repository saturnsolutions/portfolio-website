About me

Hello, I'm Aria — a designer with a soft spot for systems thinking.

For the past 7 years I've been helping startups and growing teams turn fuzzy ideas into beautiful, usable products. My background mixes graphic design, behavioral psychology, and a tiny bit of front-end code.

When I'm not in Figma, you'll find me wandering Tokyo's stationery shops, tending to my (slightly chaotic) houseplant collection, or hosting friends for slow Sunday brunches.
